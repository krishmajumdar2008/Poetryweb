Echoes of us

In the quiet of the night, I pen this verse,
A whisper to the past, a gentle curse.
Memories linger, like a haunting song,
Of a love once bright, now gone so long.

Your smile, a beacon in the darkest hours,
A fleeting fragrance of forgotten flowers.
We danced in dreams, beneath the moonâ€™s soft light,
Two hearts entwined, in the velvet night.

Time has flown, yet I still recall,
The way we loved, the rise and fall.
Your touch, a ghost that lingers on my skin,
A bittersweet reminder of what could have been.

The laughter we shared, echoes in the breeze,
Moments frozen, memories that tease.
Your voice, a melody that haunts my days,
A symphony of love, in a thousand ways.

Though you're gone, your spirit stays,
In every dawn, in every maze.
A love that's lost, yet never fades,
In the quiet nights, in the twilight shades.

So here I write, with a heart so full,
Of a bygone love, so beautiful.
In these words, may you find a trace,
Of the lover who still seeks your grace.
