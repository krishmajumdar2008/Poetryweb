Whispers of the Park

In the park where shadows softly dance,
We sit on benches, lost in trance.
The world fades out, it’s just us two,
In this serene, enchanted view.

Leaves whisper secrets, gentle and low,
Beneath the trees where memories grow.
Your laughter, a melody, pure and sweet,
Our hearts in rhythm, a steady beat.

Your hand in mine, a perfect fit,
In this moment, where dreams are lit.
Eyes meet, a spark, a silent vow,
Forever starts with here and now.

The sun dips low, a golden hue,
Painting our love in shades of blue.
Every word, every sigh, a tender mark,
Our story unfolds in whispers of the park.

With you, my bestie, love so true,
Every second feels brand new.
In this park, on these benches, we find,
A love that’s endless, pure, and kind.
