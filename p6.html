Eternal Flame

In the echoes of our past, where memories softly dwell,
I find the words I long to speak, in love’s enduring spell.
Through years that weave a tapestry, of moments shared and true,
My heart still beats a rhythm that forever calls to you.

We’ve walked through countless seasons, seen the sun and rain,
In laughter and in sorrow, you’ve eased my every pain.
Your smile, a gentle beacon, guiding me through time,
In the story of my life, you’re every cherished rhyme.

The days have turned to decades, yet my love has never waned,
For in the depth of your embrace, my soul has ever gained.
Your touch, a whispered promise, that time cannot erase,
With every kiss, a memory, in our eternal space.

Though life has brought its changes, and paths we didn’t plan,
Our love has been a constant, a bond that always stands.
In the quiet of the evening, when the world fades away,
I look into your eyes and see a love that’s here to stay.

So here’s to us, my dearest, in this journey that we share,
A love that’s stood the test of time, beyond compare.
In every beat, in every breath, my heart belongs to you,
For ours is a love eternal, a flame that burns anew.
